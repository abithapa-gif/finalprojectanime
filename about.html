Our origin story

About Anime Vault

Anime Vault was founded in 2019 by a group of lifelong anime fans who felt that most anime sites were either too casual or too focused on merchandise. We wanted something different — a deep, passionate fan space dedicated to the stories and characters that changed our lives.

We cover five of the greatest anime series ever made: Naruto, One Piece, Attack on Titan, Demon Slayer, and Fairy Tail. Each series gets the attention it deserves with episode breakdowns, character analysis, power system deep dives, and community rankings.

Whether you're a longtime Naruto fan still recovering from the Pain arc, a One Piece theorist mapping the Void Century, or a newcomer to Demon Slayer who just discovered Breathing Styles — you belong here.

No gatekeeping. No spoilers without warning. Just people who genuinely love anime, talking about anime.
